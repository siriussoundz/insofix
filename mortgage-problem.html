Mortgage Declined Because of Spray Foam Insulation?

Many UK mortgage lenders will not approve loans on homes where spray foam insulation covers roof rafters. This prevents surveyors from inspecting the roof structure for moisture damage or timber decay.

If a surveyor cannot inspect the roof structure properly, lenders may refuse to approve a mortgage or remortgage.

Insofix specialises in professional spray foam insulation removal to restore access to roof timbers and allow surveyors to complete proper inspections.

Common Situations We Help With

  • Mortgage declined due to spray foam insulation
  • Surveyor unable to inspect roof structure
  • Property sale delayed or cancelled
  • Remortgage blocked by lender
