Why Spray Foam Can Cause Mortgage Problems

Many UK mortgage lenders will not approve loans where spray foam insulation covers roof rafters. This prevents surveyors from inspecting the roof structure for damage or moisture.

Our specialist removal process restores visibility of the roof timbers, helping surveyors complete inspections and allowing property sales or remortgages to proceed.

Our Removal Process

  • Initial loft inspection
  • Careful manual spray foam removal
  • Cleaning of rafters and roof structure
  • Waste removal and safe disposal
  • Documentation for surveyors and lenders
