Why Spray Foam Insulation Can Cause Mortgage Problems

Many UK mortgage lenders will not approve loans on homes where spray foam insulation covers roof rafters. This prevents surveyors from inspecting the roof structure for damage, moisture or timber decay.

Insofix specialises in professional spray foam removal, allowing surveyors to properly inspect the roof and helping property sales or remortgages proceed.
