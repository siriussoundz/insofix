Why Spray Foam Insulation Can Cause Mortgage Problems

Many UK mortgage lenders will not approve loans on homes where spray foam insulation covers roof rafters. This can prevent surveyors from properly inspecting the roof structure for timber decay, damp, or hidden damage.

Insofix specialises in professional spray foam removal, helping restore access to the roof structure so surveyors can inspect the property properly and sales or remortgages can move forward.
