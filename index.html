Why Spray Foam Insulation Causes Mortgage Problems

Many UK mortgage lenders will not approve loans on homes where spray foam insulation covers roof rafters. Surveyors must be able to inspect the roof structure to assess timber condition, moisture damage and ventilation.

If rafters are covered in foam this inspection becomes impossible, which can lead to mortgages being declined or property sales falling through.
