Our Spray Foam Removal Process

Spray foam insulation removal requires careful manual techniques to protect the roof structure while restoring visibility of rafters and roof timbers.

Typical Removal Steps

  • Initial loft inspection
  • Assessment of foam coverage
  • Careful manual foam removal
  • Cleaning of rafters and roof structure
  • Waste removal and disposal
  • Final inspection
